INTERNSHIP HAL Dept. of CSE(AI&ML), GAT 2025-26 Page 1 INTRODUCTION This internship report outlines the work I carried out during my one -month internship at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) from 10/09/2025 to 09/10/2025. As a Seventh Semester B.E student in Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML) at Global Academy of Technology, the internship gave me valuable exposure to real -world industrial processes, defense aviation systems, networking concepts, and IT infrastructure management within a premier aerospace organization. ORGANIZATION PROFILE Hindustan Aeronautics L imited is one of India’s leading aerospace and defense companies, operating under the Ministry of Defence. Established in 1940, HAL is involved in the design, development, manufacture, and maintenance of aircraft, helicopters, avionics, and aerospace components. The Helicopter Division in Bengaluru focuses on major platforms such as the Advanced Light Helicopter (ALH), Light Combat Helicopter (LCH), and other rotary -wing aircraft for defense services. The IT Department plays a key role in integrating automation, networking, data management, and digital systems into aerospace operations. INTERNSHIP DETAILS • Organization: Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) • Division: Helicopter Division, IT Department • Duration: 10/09/2025 to 09/10/2025 (1 Month) • Role: Intern • Reporting Authority: Shri Ravishankar, Chief Manager (IT), Helicopter Division • Location: HAL Helicopter Division, Bengaluru
